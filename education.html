SK SERI REJO SARI

2011-2016

I started my elementary school in SK Seri Rejo Sari at seven years old. I was exposed to the basic element of education throughout the five years there, including language, science, math and arts. I was once an assistant class leader and school prefect.

SMK TUNKU MAHMOOD ISKANDAR

2017-2021

After that, I moved on to secondary school at SMK Tunku Mahmood Iskandar starting from age 13 until 17. At this school, I not only focused on academics but also participated in extracurricular activities such as uniform footwear competition and some leadership programs. During this time, I have held several positions such as assistant class monitor for 2 years, PRS (Pembimbing Rakan Sebaya) and SLB (Student Leader Board) both for 3 years. I also successfully completed my SPM at SMK Tunku Mahmood Iskandar, marking the end of my secondary school journey.

UiTM SEGAMAT

2022-PRESENT

I chose to continue my education at UiTM Segamat after finishing high school, enrolling in the Diploma in Information Management program. I got the chance to expand my understanding of information management at UiTM Segamat, where I studied data handling, technology, and systems that are essential in the modern digital world. I gained academic information and practical skills from the curriculum that would be crucial to my future profession.
